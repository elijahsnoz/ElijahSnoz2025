# Elijah Snoz - Art & Music 2025 🎨🎵

Welcome to the official repository for Elijah Snoz's personal website!  
This project showcases Elijah's exceptional talents in art and music, featuring a gallery of artworks, a music player for tracks, event updates, and links to shop for exclusive merchandise.

---

## 🌟 Features
- 🎵 **Music Section**: Listen to Elijah's latest tracks, including the upcoming *Do It My Way*.
- 🎨 **Art Gallery**: Explore Elijah's unique artworks, including the renowned piece *Only One*.
- 📅 **Events**: Stay updated on upcoming exhibitions and performances.
- 📱 **Social Media Integration**: Connect with Elijah on Instagram, Facebook, and X.
- 🛍️ **Shop** (Coming Soon): Get exclusive merchandise.
