About Me

I'm Elijah Snoz, a multidisciplinary artist and musician blending creativity, innovation, and passion in every piece I create.

My journey spans across visual arts, music production, and digital storytelling. I believe in pushing boundaries and creating experiences that resonate with the soul. From canvas to sound waves, every creation tells a story of dedication, emotion, and artistic evolution.

Ajayi VII

Without You

"Without You" captures the raw emotion of loss, reflection, and resilience. Blending soulful melodies with heartfelt lyricism, Elijah Snoz delivers an intimate performance that explores the space between heartbreak and healing. Each line echoes the vulnerability of letting go, while the production wraps it in smooth, atmospheric layers that define the sound of Ajayi VII.
