About the exhibition

Atunbi means rebirth. This debut solo show gathers the work at the heart of the Atunbi movement — bold, neo-expressionist paintings born from Elijah Snoz's studio and set to the sound of Ajayi VII.

It's an invitation to step into the world and be reborn with it: 200+ original works, one universe, one room.
