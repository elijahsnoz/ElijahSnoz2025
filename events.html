Elijah Snoz at the Nike Art Gallery, Abuja

Planet-B is a world of rebirth. The collection at the heart of the Planet-B movement — bold, neo-expressionist paintings born from Elijah Snoz's studio and set to the sound of Ajayi VII — debuted at the Nike Art Gallery and remains on view there in Abuja.

Whether you missed the opening or want to return, you can arrange a private viewing or a guided tour of the works. Step into the world and be reborn with it — 200+ original pieces, one universe.

Nike Art Gallery, Abuja — visiting Elijah Snoz's work

Where can I see Elijah Snoz's paintings?

Elijah Snoz's Planet-B collection is on view at the Nike Art Gallery, Abuja, open daily from 10am to 6pm. You can book a private viewing or a guided tour using the form above.

Is Elijah Snoz's art at the Nike Art Gallery in Abuja?

Yes. Following his debut solo exhibition, Elijah Snoz's (Ajayi VII) work remains on view at the Nike Art Gallery in Abuja. Reservations for viewings and tours are welcome.

What are the viewing hours at the Nike Art Gallery, Abuja?

The collection can be viewed daily between 10am and 6pm. Entry is free; a reservation is requested so a viewing or tour can be arranged.

How do I book a tour at the Nike Art Gallery?

Use the booking form above to request a date and time between 10am and 6pm, or email elijahsnoz@gmail.com. Elijah will confirm your private viewing or guided tour of the works.
